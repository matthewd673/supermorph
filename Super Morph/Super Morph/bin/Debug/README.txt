Super Morph
------------
@matthewd673's Ludum Dare 35 entry

IMPORTANT:
Please do not separate the game (".exe") and its resources ("RESOURCES")

INSTRUCTIONS:
- Use the numbers and arrows on the menu buttons to navigate the menus
- Move with W, A, S, and D
- Shapeshift with 1, 2, and 3

GAMEPLAY
- Press 1 to become a ghost and move through rocks
- Press 2 to become an attacker and shoot where you want
- Press 3 to shield-up and gain extra health
